![](kari-out-box.jpeg)

Saw this in the fridge today, so then is it a sign?

Apparently not. Kari-Out was founded by Paul Epstein (unrelated to the infamous Epstein) and has been the premier food packaging company for the past 60 years. They care about sustainability and have fair labor practices. They’re also innovators in the field having just released the very first certified compostable sauce packet.

![](kari-out-honey.jpg)

Very unsatisfying I know. Signs are only as real as you make them to be. Classic pitfall of the yearner or the schizophrenic.

This takeout box predates me and will likely out live me. Delivered to thousands each day. My company orders food daily in fact. Yet to find it here, to have my mind notice it, now how special is that?

It’s manufactured serendipity. Touched up and color-graded in post. You must not get fooled by the dunya, by the klishta, or anything else of that sort.

Stay true and stay real young poet. Now how about you go meditate?
